Tequila Chili

This classic chili recipe turns your ordinary chili dish into an interesting and fun dish to liven up any western styled dinner. The Tequila Chili, like most chili recipes, is a rather simple dish to prepare, but the added flamboyance from the meal coming from the tequila is enough to liven up any event.

As stated, the process required to prepare this dish is not to complicated, and if my measuremnts provided are followed, you should yield about four servings in about 45 minutes of total prep and cook time. The ingredients you'll need are ground beef, Italian-style stewed tomatoes, canned light red kidney beans, one onion, cloved garlic, tomato sauce, your personal choice of tequila (I recommend a bottle of Casamigos Blanco if available), chili powder, gorund cumin, salt, and cayenne pepper (measurements will be provided in the ingredients list below).

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 can (15 ounce) of light red kidney beans
  • 1 onion
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 can (8 ounce) tomato sauce
  • 1 1/2 fluid ounces of tequila
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon of ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper

Directions:

  1. Brown your brown meat in a large pot over medium heat. While it cooks, break it up with your spoon.
  2. Drain your pot.
  3. Chop your onions and mince your garlic. Stir in your tomatoes, kidney beans, onion, garlic, tomato sauce, tequila, chili powder, cumin, salt, and cayenne pepper.
  4. After adding all your ingredients, let your pot simmer for 20 more minutes.

Huzzah! You have sucessfully prepared a full pot of Tequila Chili! Pretty easy huh? Feel free to add anything to this recipe and make it your own! Make sure to check out my Mama's Buttermilk Cornbread recipe for a nice addition to have with your chili!
