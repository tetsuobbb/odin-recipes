Tequila Chili

This classic chili recipe turns your ordinary chili dish into an interesting and fun dish to liven up any western styled dinner. The Tequila Chili, like most chili recipes, is a rather simple dish to prepare, but the added flamboyance from the meal coming from the tequila is enough to liven up any event.

As stated, the process required to prepare this dish is not to complicated, and if my measuremnts provided are followed, you should yield about four servings in about 45 minutes of total prep and cook time. The ingredients you'll need are ground beef, Italian-style stewed tomatoes, canned light red kidney beans, one onion, cloved garlic, tomato sauce, your personal choice of tequila (I recommend a bottle of Casamigos Blanco if available), chili powder, gorund cumin, salt, and cayenne pepper (measurements will be provided in the ingredients list below).
